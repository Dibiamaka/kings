ABOUT US

XRISLID AFRICA UNIVERSITY

Welcome to Xrislid Africa University, the Mind Engineering University. This institution is not your regular conventional or traditional university. It is an academy poised to empower your mind, transform your thinking, help you discover your potential, explore new horizons, and become the best version of yourself. We aim to bring out the giant inside of you and ignite your creativity and innovation. Xrislid Africa University is not just an institution; it’s a movement.

The Birthplace of The Faculty Of Mind Engineering
At Xrislid Africa University, we don’t just educate; we engineer minds. The Faculty of Mind Engineering is a revolutionary discipline that recalibrates your thinking, reshapes your perceptions, and unlocks unparalleled mental potential.

For the first time in history, we have merged:
✔ Logic & Metaphysics – The fusion of rational thought with transcendent wisdom.
✔ Neuroscience & Ancient Knowledge – The science of the brain meets the wisdom of the ages.
✔ Faith & Fact – A harmony of belief and empirical truth, creating a profound new understanding of human potential.

This is not just another faculty—it is a discipline never before seen. It is a blueprint for personal evolution. It is a breakthrough that reshapes how we think, perceive, and create reality itself.

Our campuses are located in F.C.T Abuja (Headquarters), Edo State (Benin City Campus), and Manchester City, United Kingdom (Satellite Campus). We offer both physical training and a comprehensive online masterclass, enabling students in all faculties to access quality education remotely. We offer both physical training, as well as a comprehensive online masterclass to educate students in both faculties remotely.

COURSES UNDER THE FACULTY OF MIND ENGINEERING

1. Gnothi Seauton (Know Thyself) – KT101

“He who knows himself, knows all things.”

This foundational course is a deep dive into self-discovery. You cannot change your future unless you understand how you arrived at your present moment. This course unearths your subconscious programming, allowing you to break cycles, rewrite your story, and construct a new reality.

2. Emotional Intelligence

“An Emotion out of control is a destiny out of control.”
Intelligence is not just about IQ—it’s about how you manage your emotions. This course ensures that no matter how brilliant or creative you are, you develop the right mental resilience to navigate challenges, pressure, and uncertainty. It’s not just about thinking clearly—it’s about thinking powerfully under all circumstances.

3. Mindset Transformation – Rewiring Thought Patterns

“Cogitatio Creat” – Your thoughts create your reality.
Are you stuck in cycles of self-doubt, negativity, or limitation? This course is a mental reset, shifting you from:

✔ Negative to Positive Thinking

✔ Fixed to Growth Mindset

✔ Victim to Victor Mentality

✔ Adversity to Advantage

Here, you reshape your perception of life itself, unlocking powerful, unstoppable belief systems.

4. Mindset Magnetism – The Science of Attraction
“Cogito, ergo sum” – I think, therefore I am.
The greatest forces in the universe are not seen—they are felt, believed, and created by the mind. This course teaches you the principles of thought manifestation. The power of the mind is the greatest force in the universe. This course teaches you how to shape your reality through your thoughts, imagination, and focus. Learn the secrets of manifesting success, attracting opportunities, and building the future you desire—all by mastering the power of Mindset Magnetism.

5. Psychological Proficiency in Perception

“Mens regit corpus” – The mind governs the body.

A deep neurological and esoteric course, this class focuses on expanding neural activity, controlling brain waves, and reconstructing neural pathways to break free from addictions, mental constraints, and involuntary behaviors. It’s a fusion of ancient wisdom and modern neuroscience, teaching you how to gain total control over your mind and body.

This course is neuroscience meets esoteric wisdom. Here, you will learn:

✔ How to Rewire Your Brain & Create New Neural Pathways

✔ How to Manipulate Brain Waves for Maximum Performance

✔ How to Break Free from Addictions & Unwanted Behaviors

✔ How to Control Automatic Responses & Redefine Your Perception of Reality

This is not just a course—it is psychological re-engineering, giving you absolute control over mind, body, and destiny.
