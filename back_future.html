Back To The Future

Back the Future: Empower Minds, Transform Lives in Africa

Welcome to the University of the Future – Xrislid University in Nigeria, where we embark on a groundbreaking journey of Mind Engineering. Our mission is to cultivate a generation of creators, innovators, and visionaries in Africa. We believe in the transformative power of education, specifically the Education of the Mind. At our university, we are rewriting the narrative by offering free education to all, breaking down barriers that hinder intellectual growth. Our commitment to fostering a renewed, revamped, and re-engineered mindset is at the core of our vision for a brighter future in Africa. Your support is instrumental in sustaining our non-profit organization. By contributing to the University of the Future, you become a catalyst for positive change, helping us empower minds and uplift communities. Together, let's create a better world through the education of the mind. Join us in this transformative journey, where every donation becomes a beacon of hope and opportunity. Your generosity will sustain our non-profit university, ensuring that the flame of knowledge continues to burn brightly for generations to come. Let’s make education a universal right, empower minds, and build a brighter future for Africa together. Your support is not just a donation; it’s an investment in the potential of countless individuals yearning to make a difference. Together, let’s rewrite the story of education in Africa and inspire a legacy of greatness.

“The True Education is the Education of the Mind”
