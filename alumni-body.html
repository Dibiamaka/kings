Alunmi Network

At Xrislid, our commitment goes beyond education and training; we have a robust & formidable community and network to support continuous mind development even after certification and graduation.

Xrislid has educated and graduated over 10,000+ students across diverse fields of study, offering online, virtual, and physical classes at campuses in Benin City and FCT Abuja, as well as in prisons and correctional facilities.

Each graduate has their own Kofa portal login with their matric number. Need help with your login portal? Reach out to the portal admin via +2348029196199.
