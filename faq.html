FAQ

What is Mind Engineering University?

Mind Engineering University is a transformative institution dedicated to nurturing personal growth, innovation, and leadership skills. Our innovative Mind Engineering program empowers individuals to navigate life’s challenges, fostering holistic development.

What programs does Mind Engineering University offer?

We offer a comprehensive Mind Engineering program that includes sub-courses such as know thyself(KT101), Emotional Intelligence (EI202), Mindset Transformation (MT303), Mindset Magnetism(MT404), Psychological Proficiency in Perception (PPP505). These courses are designed to equip individuals with essential life skills and growth oriented mindset .

How do I apply for admission?

You can apply for admission by completing the online admission form available on our website. The form will require basic personal and educational information.

Is there any cost associated with attending Mind Engineering University?

The Faculty of Mind Engineering costs ₦650,000 (six hundred and fifty thousand naira), while the Faculty of Leadership costs ₦350,000 (three hundred and fifty thousand naira). Please note that our Benin Campus offers education completely free of charge, including no tuition fees and free writing materials, as part of our humanitarian service.

What is the schedule of classes at Mind Engineering University?

Our classes are conducted every Saturday. The 3-month program accommodates individuals with busy weekday schedules, allowing them to pursue personal development without interruption.

How can I contact Mind Engineering University for more information?

You can reach out to us through the contact details provided on our website’s “Contact Us” page. Feel free to call, email, or use the provided contact form for inquiries.

Are the programs at Mind Engineering University accredited?

while we do not seek traditional accreditation, our programs are meticulously designed by experts and our institution is affiliated with NABTEB (National Business and Technical Examination Board), ensuring quality education and recognised certification.

Can I attend specific sub-courses within the Mind Engineering program?

Our Mind Engineering program is designed to offer a holistic learning experience. While you can select a specific sub-course, we recommend participating in the entire program to fully benefit from its comprehensive curriculum.

Are there any prerequisites for enrolling in the Mind Engineering program?

The Mind Engineering program is open to individuals from diverse backgrounds. There are no specific prerequisites, but a passion for personal growth and an open mindset will enhance your experience.

What sets Mind Engineering University apart from other educational institutions?

Mind Engineering University is not just an educational institution; it’s a transformative movement. Our focus on personal growth, empowerment, and community impact distinguishes us from traditional universities, creating change-makers who redefine the narrative of Africa.

What outcomes can I expect from the Mind Engineering program?

The Mind Engineering program is designed to empower you with a growth-oriented mindset, enhanced emotional intelligence, and a toolbox of life skills. Graduates often experience improved self-awareness, effective communication, and a greater ability to lead and adapt in various contexts.

Can I enroll in the Mind Engineering program if I have no prior academic background?

Absolutely! The Mind Engineering program is open to individuals from all walks of life. Our focus is on personal development, and we believe that everyone has the potential to learn and grow.

How do you ensure the quality of education at Mind Engineering University?

Our programs are curated by experts with extensive experience in personal development, leadership, and related fields. We emphasize practical application and interactive learning to ensure that you gain skills that are relevant and valuable.

Are there opportunities for networking and community engagement?

Yes, Mind Engineering University fosters a vibrant community of like-minded individuals. Through workshops, events, and collaborative projects, you’ll have ample opportunities to connect, share experiences, and learn from fellow participants.

Can I apply for multiple sub-courses within the Mind Engineering program?

While you can express your interest in multiple sub-courses, we recommend selecting the one that resonates most with your goals and aspirations. Each sub-course is designed to provide a comprehensive learning experience.

What is the age range for applicants to the Mind Engineering program?

Our program is open to individuals aged 18 and above. We welcome individuals from different age groups who are passionate about personal growth and positive change.

How are the Mind Engineering sessions conducted?

Our sessions blend interactive workshops, discussions, practical exercises, and experiential learning. While predominantly held on Saturdays, we ensure a dynamic and engaging learning environment.

Are there opportunities for alumni engagement after completing the program?

Yes, we encourage alumni engagement through exclusive events, networking opportunities, and continued learning experiences. Our alumni community is a valuable resource for ongoing growth and connection.

Is the Mind Engineering program only for residents of Africa?

While our primary focus is on empowering individuals in Africa, our program is open to participants from around the world who are committed to personal growth and positive impact.

How can I contribute to the community impact initiatives of Mind Engineering University?

We welcome your involvement in our community impact projects and initiatives. You can participate as a volunteer, collaborator, or supporter to help us make a positive difference in society.

Are there online courses at Xrislid Mind Engineering University?

yes, Xrislid University offers online classes. These classes cover all aspects of all The Mind Engineering Program.

2. Can I apply for a job with the certificate from Xrislid University?

Xrislid is not a degree issuing institution. instead, we provide two certificates, a NABTEB certificate and a certificate of completion. These certificate can enhance your CV and portfolio. Serving as valuable credentials to boost your professional profile.
