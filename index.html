ABOUT US

XRISLID AFRICA UNIVERSITY

The World’s First Faculty of Mind Engineering,
Where Thought Becomes Power, and Power Shapes Reality

Welcome to Xrislid Africa University, the Mind Engineering University. This institution is not your regular conventional or traditional university. It is an academy poised to empower your mind, transform your thinking, help you discover your potential, explore new horizons, and become the best version of yourself. We aim to bring out the giant inside of you and ignite your creativity and innovation. Xrislid Africa University is not just an institution; it’s a movement.

The Birthplace of The Faculty Of Mind Engineering
At Xrislid Africa University, we don’t just educate; we engineer minds. The Faculty of Mind Engineering is a revolutionary discipline that recalibrates your thinking, reshapes your perceptions, and unlocks unparalleled mental potential.

For the first time in history, we have merged:
✔ Logic & Metaphysics – The fusion of rational thought with transcendent wisdom.
✔ Neuroscience & Ancient Knowledge – The science of the brain meets the wisdom of the ages.
✔ Faith & Fact – A harmony of belief and empirical truth, creating a profound new understanding of human potential.

This is not just another faculty—it is a discipline never before seen. It is a blueprint for personal evolution. It is a breakthrough that reshapes how we think, perceive, and create reality itself.

Our campuses are located in F.C.T Abuja (Headquarters), Edo State (Benin City Campus), and Manchester City, United Kingdom (Satellite Campus). We offer both physical training and a comprehensive online masterclass, enabling students in all faculties to access quality education remotely. We offer both physical training, as well as a comprehensive online masterclass to educate students in both faculties remotely.
